## AI Prompt for Generating a Modern Fashion Frontend

Design a premium, modern, and responsive fashion e-commerce homepage UI.

Requirements:

- Clean luxury design with white background and subtle shadows.
- Hero banner with "New Collection 2026".
- Search bar at the top.
- Category section displayed as responsive cards with rounded corners.
- Each category should include a high-quality icon or image and category name.

Main Categories:

1. Clothing
2. Footwear
3. Watches
4. Jewelry
5. Bags
6. Accessories
7. Beauty
8. Travel & Lifestyle

Each category card should have:

- Large image
- Category title
- Product count
- Hover animation
- "Explore" button

Below the categories include:

- Featured Products
- New Arrivals
- Best Sellers
- Trending Products
- Brands Section
- Customer Reviews
- Newsletter Subscription
- Footer with social links

Style:

- Luxury brands like Zara, Gucci, Nike, and Apple Store.
- Rounded cards (16px radius)
- Smooth animations
- Mobile-first responsive layout
- Grid system with 4 cards per row on desktop, 2 on tablet, and 1 on mobile.
- Use modern typography (Inter or Poppins).
- Elegant black, white, and gold color palette.
